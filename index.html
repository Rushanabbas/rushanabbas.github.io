What is AutoAdz

Advertise that moves with the city

AutoAds is a hyperlocal out-of-home (OOH) advertising platform that transforms auto rickshaws into moving billboards. Unlike static hoardings, our ads travel through busy streets, local markets, and residential areas—reaching real people where they live and commute. We help brands achieve high visibility, better recall, and real-world presence without digital ad fatigue

We manage a network of thousands of branded autos across India's fastest-growing cities, offering brands unmatched local visibility, campaign tracking, and zero digital ad fatigue.

Autoadz Aim

Autoadz aim is to make advertising more local, visible, and effective by taking it directly onto the streets. Instead of relying on static billboards or crowded online ads, the goal is to help businesses reach real people in their daily environment—while they commute, shop, and move around the city. In simple terms, the aim of AutoAds is: To turn everyday auto rickshaws into powerful moving advertising spaces To give brands hyperlocal reach in areas traditional ads can't cover To provide affordable marketing for both small businesses and big brands To create a system where ads are constantly moving, increasing visibility and recall And to bring more transparency and tracking into offline advertising.
